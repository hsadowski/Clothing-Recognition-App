Our Story

At Tomato Bar Pizza Bakery, we believe in creating exceptional craft pizzas using only the freshest ingredients and traditional techniques. Our dough is made fresh daily, our sauces are crafted in-house, and our toppings are carefully selected to ensure the highest quality.

What started as a small pizza shop has grown into a beloved local institution with three locations across Northwest Indiana.
