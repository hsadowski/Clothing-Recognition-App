Our Story

Crafting delicious pizza with passion since 2010

How It All Began

Tomato Bar Pizza Bakery was born from a simple idea: create amazing craft pizza using the freshest ingredients and traditional techniques. What started as a small pizza shop in Valparaiso has grown into a beloved local institution with three locations across Northwest Indiana.

Our founders, Mike and Sarah, met in culinary school and shared a passion for artisanal pizza. After years of working in high-end restaurants, they decided to bring their culinary expertise to their hometown. They spent months perfecting their dough recipe, sourcing the best ingredients, and creating unique flavor combinations that would set Tomato Bar apart.

In 2010, they opened the first Tomato Bar in Valparaiso, quickly gaining a reputation for exceptional pizza and a welcoming atmosphere. The success of the original location led to expansion, with Schererville opening in 2015 and Crown Point in 2018.

Today, Tomato Bar continues to be a family-owned business committed to quality, creativity, and community. We're proud to be a part of Northwest Indiana's vibrant food scene and grateful to our loyal customers who have supported us along the way.

Mike and Sarah, Founders of Tomato Bar Pizza Bakery

Our Values

Fresh Ingredients

We source the freshest ingredients from local suppliers whenever possible. Our dough is made fresh daily, our sauces are crafted in-house, and our toppings are carefully selected to ensure the highest quality.

Handcrafted Quality

Every pizza is handcrafted with care and attention to detail. We believe in traditional techniques and take pride in the artisanal quality of our food. No shortcuts, no compromises.

Creative Innovation

We're constantly experimenting with new flavors and combinations to create unique pizza experiences. Our specialty pizzas showcase our creative approach to traditional favorites.

Community Focus

We're proud to be part of the Northwest Indiana community. We support local events, partner with area businesses, and strive to create a welcoming atmosphere for everyone.

Sustainability

We're committed to reducing our environmental impact through sustainable practices, including eco-friendly packaging, waste reduction, and energy-efficient equipment.

Inclusivity

We believe everyone should be able to enjoy great pizza. That's why we offer a variety of options for different dietary needs, including vegan and gluten-free choices.

Our Pizza-Making Process

1

The Perfect Dough

Our journey begins with our signature dough, made fresh daily using a blend of high-quality flours, filtered water, sea salt, and a touch of olive oil. The dough ferments for 24 hours to develop its distinctive flavor and texture.

2

Handcrafted Sauces

Our sauces are made in-house using San Marzano tomatoes, fresh herbs, and a blend of spices. From our classic red sauce to our specialty bases like garlic oil, pesto, and buffalo, each sauce is crafted to complement our toppings perfectly.

3

Premium Toppings

We use only the finest ingredients for our toppings, from premium cheeses to fresh vegetables and quality meats. Many of our specialty ingredients are prepared in-house, like our roasted tomatoes and garlic-grilled portobellos.

4

Baked to Perfection

Each pizza is carefully assembled by hand and baked in our stone-deck ovens at the perfect temperature. The result is a pizza with a crispy yet chewy crust, melted cheese, and perfectly cooked toppings.
