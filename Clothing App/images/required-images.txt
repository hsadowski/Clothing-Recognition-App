## Image Specifications

### Logo
- Main logo: 200px wide, transparent background
- White logo: 200px wide, transparent background

### Hero Images
- Hero backgrounds: 1920px × 1080px, high-quality food photography

### Menu Item Images
- Menu items: 500px × 350px, consistent lighting and styling
- All food should be professionally photographed on a consistent background

### Location Images
- Location exteriors: 800px × 600px
- Location interiors: 800px × 600px
- Thumbnails: 200px × 150px

### App Images
- App mockup: 300px wide, showing the app on a smartphone
- App store badges: Standard sizes from Apple and Google

### Map Marker
- 40px × 40px, PNG with transparency

## Image Guidelines
1. All images should be optimized for web (compressed without visible quality loss)
2. Use consistent styling across all photography
3. Food images should look appetizing and professional
4. Location images should be well-lit and showcase the atmosphere
5. Use descriptive filenames with hyphens between words
6. All images should include appropriate alt text in the HTML
